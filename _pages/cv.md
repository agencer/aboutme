## Selected information

**Alper Sukru Gencer**  
Ph.D. Candidate in Political Science  
New York University

My research focuses on democratic backsliding, political behavior, affective polarization, crisis politics, and political accountability. I use survey experiments, causal inference, and formal theory to study how citizens evaluate complex political choices under uncertainty.
